It always falls between -1 and 1, and it will be positive for call options and negative for put options. The reason for this should be intuitive if you examine the payoff diagrams for call and put options.

For call options, delta approaches 1 as the underlying's price goes to infinity, and it approaches 0 as the underlying's price approaches 0. For put options, it approaches -1 as the underlying's price goes to 0 and it approaches 0 as the underlying's price approaches infinity
